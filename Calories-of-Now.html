Mindful Eating in the Present Moment

Traditional calorie counting keeps you stuck in the past or anxious about the future. The Calories of Now brings you back to the present moment, helping you focus on what truly matters: the food choices you're making right now.

Our revolutionary approach eliminates the stress of traditional tracking by giving you real-time guidance on your caloric needs. No more spreadsheets, no more guilt, no more overthinking. Just simple, mindful nutrition.

Whether you're maintaining your ideal weight, losing fat, or building muscle, The Calories of Now helps you stay on track without the mental burden of traditional calorie counting.

How to Use the App

Simple steps to transform your relationship with food

1

Set Your Goals

Enter your current weight, target weight, and timeframe. The app calculates your optimal daily caloric intake based on your goals.

2

Check Your Budget

Open the app anytime to see how many calories you can have right now. The number updates in real-time based on your pace for the day.

3

Log Your Meals

Quickly log what you eat. The app automatically adjusts your remaining calorie budget for the rest of the day.

4

Stay Present

Focus on making the best choice right now. Don't worry about yesterday's mistakes or tomorrow's challenges. Just be present.

5

Adjust as Needed

If you go over or under, the app automatically recalculates for the rest of your day. No stress, no guiltâ€”just guidance.

6

Reach Your Goals

By staying present with your nutrition day after day, you'll naturally reach your ideal weight without the mental burden of traditional dieting.
